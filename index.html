Welcome to Team Flare

At Team Flare, we are more than just a robotics team – we are innovators, problem solvers, and passionate creators. Join us on our journey as we redefine the future of robotics and engineering, one robot at a time. Let's work together to make a lasting impact in the world of STEM.

About Us

We are Team Flare, a dedicated VEX Robotics team based in Frisco, Texas. We are passionate about designing, building, and competing with innovative robots.

Every year, VEX Robotics challenges students to build and program robots that tackle dynamic, real-world problems. Our team uses the VEX V5 Robotics platform to design robots capable of solving complex challenges, compete at the highest level, and innovate beyond the ordinary.

What is VEX V5 Robotics?

VEX V5 Robotics is the cutting-edge platform empowering the next generation of engineers and innovators. With its powerful motors, sensors, and versatile construction elements, VEX V5 provides the foundation for creating robots that are not only competitive but also scalable for real-world engineering applications. Our team uses VEX V5 to design robots capable of solving complex challenges, compete at the highest level, and innovate beyond the ordinary.

Every year, VEX Robotics challenges students to build and program robots that tackle dynamic, real-world problems. With VEX V5, we gain the experience, hands-on skills, and technical knowledge needed to excel in robotics competitions, pushing us closer to shaping the future of engineering.
